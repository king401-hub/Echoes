The Echoes (TE)

Empowering Children Through Expressive Arts

Who We Are

The Echoes is a literary organization with a passion for discovering, nurturing, and promoting the artistic talents of children. Our focus is on the expressive arts—poetry, drama, acting, and storytelling. These are powerful tools through which young minds can find their voices and share their unique perspectives with the world.

We believe that every child is a world of potential! Our mission is to provide platforms where children can confidently express themselves, explore their creativity, and be celebrated for their individuality. It is our utmost joy to see kids shine—unfiltered, unapologetically themselves, and proud of their talent.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of confident, expressive, and creatively empowered children who are not afraid to let their voices echo across the world.

Our Mission

To discover, harness, and showcase the innate artistic abilities of children by providing them with platforms and opportunities to express their creative gifts.

What We Do

Every year, The Echoes hosts one of our signature events:

  • Poetry Festivals
  • Drama Competitions
  • Story Writing Contests

Each event is carefully curated to allow children to step into the spotlight, build confidence, and connect with others through the beauty of words and performance.

Accolades and monetary prizes are awarded to outstanding participants—recognizing excellence, encouraging passion, and inspiring every child to keep dreaming, creating, and expressing.

Why “The Echoes”?

The name Echoes symbolizes our core belief: that every child’s voice deserves to resonate, to be heard, and to leave a lasting impact. We don’t just give children a stage, we amplify their voices. Every child is a message the world needs to hear.

Join Us

Whether you’re a parent, teacher, artist, or supporter of the arts, we invite you to join us in making sure no child’s voice goes unheard. At The Echoes, every child matters and every child has a story worth telling.
